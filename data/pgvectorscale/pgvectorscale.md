## Overview

PGVectorScale is a suite of tooling developed by TimescaleDB to enhance and scale vector search capabilities in PostgreSQL. It extends PGVector with sharding and performance optimizations specifically designed for AI workloads.

## Features

- Scales PostgreSQL + PGVector for AI applications
- Sharding support for distributed vector search
- Performance optimizations for large vector datasets
- TimescaleDB integration and expertise
