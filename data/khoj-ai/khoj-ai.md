## Overview

Khoj AI is a fully self-hostable personal AI assistant that serves as your second brain. It integrates with both online and local LLMs to provide autonomous capabilities.

## Features

- Self-hostable for complete privacy
- Answer questions from the web or your own documents
- Build custom agents for specialized tasks
- Schedule automations
- Deep research capabilities
- Support for multiple LLMs: GPT, Claude, Gemini, Llama, Qwen, Mistral
- Personal and autonomous operation
- Web-based and API interfaces
