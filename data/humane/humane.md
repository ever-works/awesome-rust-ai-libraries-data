## Overview

Humane's AI Pin is a wearable multi-modal device designed to provide ambient AI computing capabilities with privacy-focused digital assistants.

## Features

- Wearable form factor
- Multi-modal input/output
- Ambient computing capabilities
- Suite of AI digital assistants
- Privacy-first design
- Human-centered experience

## Use Cases

- Hands-free AI assistance
- Ambient information access
- Task automation on the go
