## Overview

Hugging Face Tokenizers is a Rust-based library providing fast tokenization for transformer models. It implements Byte-Pair Encoding (BPE), WordPiece, and other tokenization algorithms with exceptional performance, and is the foundation of tokenization across the Hugging Face ecosystem.

## Features

- High-performance BPE and WordPiece tokenization in Rust
- Used for GPT-2, BERT, RoBERTa, and many other models
- Pre-tokenization and post-processing pipelines
- Training tokenizers from scratch on custom corpora
- Bindings available for Python (via PyO3) while implemented in Rust

## Status

Active development, widely used in production.
