## Overview

SimCourt is a benchmark and framework for simulating court proceedings using LLM-based agents. It evaluates agents' capabilities in legal reasoning, argumentation, and judicial process simulation.

## Features

- Court simulation framework using LLM agents
- Evaluates legal reasoning capabilities
- Multi-agent judicial simulation
- Benchmark for legal AI assessment
- Standardized evaluation protocol for court simulation

## Use Cases

- Benchmarking legal reasoning in LLM agents
- Simulating court proceedings with AI agents
- Research on legal AI evaluation
