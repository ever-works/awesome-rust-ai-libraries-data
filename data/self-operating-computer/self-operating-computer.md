## Overview

Self Operating Computer (SOC) enables multimodal models to autonomously interact with a computer using human-like inputs and outputs.

## Features

- Multimodal model compatibility
- GPT-4v, Gemini Pro Vision, and LLaVA support
- Keyboard and mouse control
- Voice mode support
- Optical character recognition (OCR)
- Future support for additional models

## Pricing

Free and open-source.
