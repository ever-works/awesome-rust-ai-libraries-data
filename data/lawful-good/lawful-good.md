## Overview

Lawful-Good is a benchmark designed to assess the legal capabilities of LLM agents. It evaluates agents' proficiency in legal reasoning, statutory interpretation, regulatory compliance, and other law-related tasks.

## Features

- Legal capability assessment for LLM agents
- Tests legal reasoning and interpretation
- Evaluates compliance analysis abilities
- Standardized legal task benchmarking
- Measures domain-specific legal knowledge

## Use Cases

- Benchmarking legal AI agents
- Evaluating LLM legal reasoning capabilities
- Research on legal domain evaluation
