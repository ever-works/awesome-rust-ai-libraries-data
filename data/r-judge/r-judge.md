## Overview

R-Judge is a benchmark presented at EMNLP Findings 2024 for evaluating safety risk awareness in LLM agents. It assesses agents' ability to identify, understand, and respond appropriately to potential safety hazards across various task scenarios.

## Features

- Safety risk awareness evaluation for LLM agents
- Published at EMNLP Findings 2024
- Assesses hazard identification capabilities
- Evaluates appropriate risk response behaviors
- Standardized safety benchmarking methodology

## Use Cases

- Evaluating agent safety awareness
- Benchmarking risk assessment capabilities
- Research on safe agent behavior
