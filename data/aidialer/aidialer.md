## Overview

Aidialer is a comprehensive application for building AI-powered phone call systems with interruptible, low-latency, near-human quality conversations.

## Features

- Interruptible voice interactions
- Near-human quality speech synthesis
- Low-latency response processing
- Built with LangChain, Hugging Face STT/TTS models
- Full stack application for phone-based AI conversations

## Pricing

Free and open-source.
