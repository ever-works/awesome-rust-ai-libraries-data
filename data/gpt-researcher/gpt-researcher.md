## Overview

GPT Researcher is an autonomous AI agent designed to conduct comprehensive online research and produce detailed, unbiased reports.

## Features

- Autonomous online research capabilities
- Unbiased research methodology
- Detailed report generation
- Advanced web scraping integration
- Optimized for speed, reliability, and cost-effectiveness
- Leverages recent AI advancements for improved research quality

## Use Cases

- Academic and market research
- Competitive intelligence gathering
- Comprehensive topic investigation
- Automated report generation
