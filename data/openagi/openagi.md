## Overview

OpenAGI by AGI Research is an open-source platform integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) with domain-specific expert models for complex task-solving.

## Features

- LLM integration with domain-specific expert models
- Reinforcement Learning from Task Feedback (RLTF) mechanism
- Self-improvement capabilities
- Paradigm where LLMs operate various external models
- Complex task-solving support

## Pricing

Free and open-source.
