## Overview

Botpress is an open-source platform for building, deploying, and managing conversational AI agents. It provides a visual flow builder and supports integration with various LLMs including GPT models.

## Features

- Visual drag-and-drop conversation flow builder
- Support for multiple LLM backends (GPT, Claude, etc.)
- Built-in NLU and intent recognition
- Multi-channel deployment (web, Slack, Teams, Telegram)
- Self-hosted or cloud deployment options
- Knowledge base integration for RAG-based responses
- Analytics and conversation monitoring

## Pricing

Free and open-source under the AGPL-3.0 license for self-hosted use. Cloud-hosted plans are also available.
