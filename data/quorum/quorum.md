## Overview

Quorum is a multi-agent AI system designed for structured debates and collaborative discussion, supporting multiple discussion methods and model backends.

## Features

- 7 discussion methods: Standard, Oxford, Socratic, Delphi, Brainstorm, Tradeoff, and Advocate
- Multi-phase consensus: independent answers, critique, discussion, and synthesis
- Supports Claude, GPT, Gemini, Grok, and local Ollama models
- Terminal UI with real-time streaming
- Auto-discovery of local Ollama models
