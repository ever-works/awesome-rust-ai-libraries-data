## Overview

Sakana AI's evolutionary model merge (EMM) combines open-source models using evolutionary techniques.

## Features

- Combines 500,000 open-source models
- Evolutionary techniques for model fusion
- Groundbreaking benchmark results
- Creates new foundation models
- Not explicitly optimized for specific benchmarks
- Empowers AI with combined knowledge

## Vision

A significant step toward AGI, akin to Retrieval Augmented Generation.
