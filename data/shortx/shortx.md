## Overview

ShortX by RayVentura is an AI-powered video automation platform for short-form video content across social media platforms.

## Features

- YouTube Shorts support
- Instagram Reels support
- TikTok integration
- Snapchat compatibility
- Customizable templates
- AI-powered services
- Subscription model
- Affiliate program
