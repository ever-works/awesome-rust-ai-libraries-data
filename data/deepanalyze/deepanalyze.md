## Overview

DeepAnalyze is an agentic LLM system designed for autonomous data science tasks without relying on predefined workflows, enabling adaptive and curriculum-based learning.

## Features

- Agentic LLM without any predefined workflow
- Autonomous orchestration and adaptive optimization
- Curriculum-based agentic training in real-world environments
- Supports data tasks: preparation, analysis, modeling, visualization, and reporting
- Supports data research: deep research across unstructured, semi-structured, and structured data
